
This dog is a big problem for his owner.

WHY
=======================================================
He barks excessively at people and other animals,
chews on furniture and shoes, and does not obey commands. 
He is always excited to see his owner, wagging his tail 
and greeting them with joy. 
He is often aggressive and tends to run away, 
creating problems and potential dangers. 
He is well-trained,
and misbehaves both at home and outside. 
He enjoys playing,
and protective of his family and home.
He requires significant training and continuous supervision.
